Pulse Logo Placeholder

To complete the Pulse branding, please add your logo to this directory with the filename:

pulse-logo.png

The logo should be:

  • A transparent PNG image
  • Approximately 200x80 pixels for optimal display
  • In the brown/orange color palette matching the Pulse theme

Once you've added the logo file, it will automatically appear in the header and other branding locations in the app.

If you don't have a logo ready, the app will gracefully handle the missing image.
